Terms and Definition

Normalized subjects - total number of subjects by different course code teach by a lecturer in a selected session and semester.
Normalized student - total number of students of all subjects teach by of a lecturer in a selected session and semester.
Normalized weekly classes - total hours of classes (timetable) in one week of a lecturer in a selected session and semester.
For this system, min-max normalization technique is used to calculate workload. The example below will describe the normalization technique used.

Calculating Normalized Subjects, Normalized Students and Normalized Weekly Classes

Lecturer Name Subjects Normalized Subjects
A 20 1.00
B 10 0.50
C 5 0.25
From the table:
The highest subjects count is 20
The lowest subjects count is 0 (there are lecturers that are teaching this semester)
To calculate the normalized subjects:
We use the formula below:
(Current Lecturer's Subject Count - Lowest Subjects Count) / Highest Subjects Count

To calculate the normalized subjects of Lecturer A:
Highest Subjects Count = 20,
Lowest Subjects Count = 0,
Lecturer A's Subject Count = 20
Normalized Subjects = (20-0)/20 = 1.00

To calculate the normalized subjects of Lecturer C:
Highest Subjects Count = 20,
Lowest Subjects Count = 0,
Lecturer C's Subject Count = 5
Normalized Subjects = (5-0)/20 = 5/20 = 0.25

Calculating Normalized Workload

Lecturer Name Subjects Normalized Subjects Students Normalized Students Weekly Classes (Hour) Normalized Weekly Classes Sum Normalized...(Hidden From Page) Overall Workload
A 20 1.00 50 0.50 10 1.00 2.50 1.00
B 10 0.50 100 1.00 5 0.50 2.50 1.00
C 5 0.25 10 0.10 5 0.50 0.85 0.34
The formula used is same as calculating normalized subjects, students and weekly classes(Sum Normalized...)
(Current Lecturer's Sum Normalized... - Lowest Sum Normalized...) / Highest Sum Normalized...

To calculate the overall workload of Lecturer A:
Highest Sum Normalized... = 2.50,
Lowest Sum Normalized... = 0.00,
Lecturer A's Sum Normalized = 2.50
Overall Workload = (2.50-0)/2.50 = 1.00

To calculate the normalized subjects of Lecturer C:
Highest Sum Normalized... = 2.50,
Lowest Sum Normalized... = 0.00,
Lecturer C's Sum Normalized... = 0.85
Normalized Subjects = (0.85-0)/2.50 = 0.34
